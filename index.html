How to Play

A round lasts as long as the market wants it to. Your nerve is the only clock that matters.

01

Pick Direction

Tap BUY 📈 if you think price rises. Tap SELL 📉 if you think it falls.

02

Set Your Risk

Drag sliders to choose your Stop Loss and Profit Target. The R:R ratio updates live.

03

Watch the Market

Candles animate live. Your P&L updates every tick. Regimes shift from Bull to Bear to Sideways.

04

Win or Hold

Let SL/TP execute automatically — or manually close at any time. Every decision is a lesson.

Review & Grow

Read the coach's commentary. Study your journal. Climb the leaderboards. Level up your career.
