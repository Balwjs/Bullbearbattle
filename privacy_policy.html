01 Overview

🛡️ Core Commitment

BullBear Battle does not collect personal information you voluntarily provide for account creation — there are no accounts. Your game progress, balance, achievements, and statistics are stored locally on your device only. We value your privacy and limit data collection to what is strictly necessary to operate the app and serve contextually relevant advertisements.

BullBear Battle is a trading simulation arcade game. No real money is involved. No financial transactions occur. This Privacy Policy applies to the BullBear Battle mobile application available on iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play).

04 How Data Is Used

We use data solely for the following purposes:

  • Game functionality: Save and restore your progress, achievements, and settings across app sessions.
  • Personalised experience: The frustration engine, coach commentary, and career progression use your locally stored statistics to adapt gameplay.
  • Advertising: To display interstitial ads via Google AdMob after every N trades. Ads are shown only after GDPR/consent is obtained where legally required.
  • App stability: Crash data may be reported by the OS (iOS CrashKit / Android Vitals) to help us fix bugs. We do not add additional crash analytics SDKs.

We do not sell, rent, share, or monetise your personal data to any third party beyond Google's advertising services described in this policy.

05 Advertising (Google AdMob)

BullBear Battle uses Google AdMob (SDK version 25.x) to display interstitial advertisements. Ads appear after a set number of completed trades.

⚠️ Consent First

In regions covered by GDPR (EU/EEA/UK) and equivalent laws, the app presents a User Messaging Platform (UMP) consent dialog on first launch. Personalised advertising is only enabled if you give explicit consent. If you decline, contextual (non-personalised) ads are shown instead.

You may review or withdraw your consent at any time via Settings → Privacy → Ad Preferences inside the app.

Google AdMob's data practices are governed by the Google Privacy Policy and the AdMob Partner Guidelines.

On Android, the com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID permission is requested solely for AdMob's use. On iOS, the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework governs access to the IDFA.

07 Data Storage & Transfers

All gameplay data is stored using Android SharedPreferences / iOS UserDefaults — both are sandboxed local storage mechanisms that cannot be accessed by other apps. Data is not transmitted to any server operated by us.

If you use iCloud Backup (iOS) or Google Backup (Android), your device's backup service may include app data as part of a full device backup. These backups are governed by Apple's and Google's respective privacy policies.

Ad-related data processed by Google AdMob may be transferred to and stored on Google's servers, which may be located outside your country of residence. Google LLC is a party to the EU-US Data Privacy Framework and provides adequate safeguards for international transfers.

08 Children's Privacy

👶 Age Requirement

BullBear Battle is intended for users aged 13 and older (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

The app contains simulated financial content (virtual trading) which is intended for educational entertainment only and is not appropriate for very young children. Parents and guardians should supervise usage.

10 GDPR, CCPA & Indian IT Act

EU/EEA/UK Users (GDPR): We process your data on the basis of legitimate interest (app functionality) and consent (personalised advertising). You have the rights described in Section 9. Our UMP consent dialog is displayed at first launch to collect your advertising consent lawfully.

California Users (CCPA/CPRA): We do not sell personal information. California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete, and the right to opt-out of sale. Since we do not sell data, no opt-out mechanism beyond the standard ad consent is required.

Indian Users (DPDP Act 2023): In compliance with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, we process only the minimum data required for app functionality. Data fiduciaries are required to implement reasonable security safeguards, which we do. Indian users have the right to access, correction, and erasure.

12 Security

We implement the following security measures to protect your data:

  • All game data is stored in OS-sandboxed local storage inaccessible to other apps.
  • No user credentials, financial data, or sensitive personal information is stored anywhere.
  • The app does not transmit gameplay data over any network connection.
  • Google AdMob communications occur over TLS-encrypted HTTPS connections.
  • The Android APK is signed with a release keystore; the iOS app is code-signed with an Apple Developer certificate.

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we continually work to protect your information using industry-standard practices.
