Getting Started

How to Play

  1. Download BullBear Battle from the App Store (requires iOS 16.0 or later).
  2. On first launch, complete the 5-screen onboarding tutorial to learn the core mechanics.
  3. You start with ₹1,00,000 in virtual capital. No real money is ever used.
  4. On the main screen, drag the Stop Loss and Target sliders to set your risk/reward.
  5. Tap BUY 📈 (go long) or SELL 📉 (go short) to enter a trade.
  6. Watch candlestick charts animate live. Your P&L updates in real time.
  7. The trade ends automatically when price hits your Stop Loss or Target — or tap CLOSE to exit manually.
  8. Review coach commentary in the result overlay, then tap Play Again.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

🎮 Gameplay
Is BullBear Battle a real trading app? Does it use real money?

No. BullBear Battle is a simulation arcade game for entertainment and education. You start with ₹1,00,000 in virtual currency. No real money is ever deposited, traded, or withdrawn. The app is not a financial product, brokerage, or investment platform.

The candlestick charts are generated by a simulated Geometric Brownian Motion price model — not live market data.

What is a "Jackpot Round" and how does it work?

Approximately every 10 completed trades, the game triggers a Jackpot Round. During a jackpot, the price generator produces candles with 3–5× the normal move strongly in your favour — giving you a chance at an outsized win.

You'll hear the jackpot sound cue when a jackpot round begins. The jackpot effect lasts for the first few candles of the trade.

What is a "Near-Miss Round"?

After 4+ consecutive wins, the frustration engine may trigger a Near-Miss. In a near-miss, price initially moves toward your target, then reverses and hits your stop loss — closely simulating the emotional experience of watching a winning trade turn into a loss.

This mechanic is intentional and teaches the importance of locking in profits with the Move Stop Loss feature.

What are Market Regimes (Bull / Bear / Sideways)?

The market cycles through three regimes every 10–30 candles: BULL ↗, BEAR ↘, and SIDEWAYS ↔. The current regime is shown as a badge on the chart.

In Bull regime, there's a slight directional bias upward. In Bear, a slight bias downward. In Sideways, movement is random. Learning to read regime shifts is part of the skill development the game teaches.

How does the "Move Stop Loss" feature work?

While a trade is active in Playing state, tap the Move SL button to open the Stop Loss sheet. You can:

• Drag the slider to a new SL price
• Use presets: Break Even, Lock 50%, Lock 75%

Moving your stop loss to break-even is a key risk management technique. The new SL cannot be moved beyond current price in the losing direction.

What happens if my balance reaches ₹0?

If your virtual balance drops to ₹0 or below, the Game Over screen appears. You can restart from your chosen Starting Balance (configurable in Settings). Your statistics and achievements are preserved — only your balance resets.

What is the Trade Journal?

Tap the 📓 icon in the top bar during gameplay to open the Trade Journal. It shows your complete trade history grouped by date, including:

• Daily P&L totals
• Mini sparkline charts for each trade
• Coach commentary with R:R analysis
• Win/loss/near-miss/manual-close labels

What does the Coach say and how is commentary generated?

After every trade, the Coach Engine generates a contextual comment based on your result, your streak, your R:R ratio, and whether you moved your stop loss. Comments appear in the result overlay and are saved in the Trade Journal.

⚙️ Settings & Profile
How do I change my starting balance, position size, or timeframe?

Go to Settings → Gameplay. Available options:

• Starting Balance: ₹50,000 / ₹1,00,000 / ₹2,00,000 / ₹5,00,000
• Position Size: ₹1,000 / ₹2,000 / ₹5,000 / ₹10,000
• Timeframe: 2 Min (Scalping) / 5 Min (Day) / 15 Min (Swing)
• Difficulty: Easy / Normal / Hard

Changes to Starting Balance take effect on the next Game Over + restart, not immediately.

How do I reset my balance, stats, or achievements?

Navigate to Settings → Danger Zone:

• Reset Statistics — clears all performance metrics, keeps balance and achievements
• Reset Achievements — resets all achievement progress, keeps balance and stats
• Reset Everything — full factory reset, restores starting balance and wipes all data

All reset actions require a confirmation dialog to prevent accidental deletion.

How do I change my Trader Name or Persona?

Go to Settings → Player Profile. You can edit your display name (shown in the header) and choose a Trader Persona: Scalper, Day Trader, Swing Trader, Whale, Gambler, or Hedge Fund. Each persona has a unique accent colour.

How do I turn off sound or haptics?

Go to Settings → Audio to toggle Sound Effects and Text-to-Speech independently. Go to Settings → Haptics to toggle vibration feedback. All changes take effect immediately.

Is there a dark mode / light mode toggle?

BullBear Battle is designed with a dark-first aesthetic optimised for trading chart readability. A light mode toggle is available in Settings → Display → Dark Theme.

🔧 Technical Issues
The app crashed / freezes on launch. What do I do?

Try these steps in order:

1. Force-quit and relaunch the app.
2. Restart your device.
3. Ensure you have the latest version of the app from App Store / Google Play.
4. Free up storage — the app requires at least 100 MB of free space.
5. If the issue persists, uninstall and reinstall the app (note: this will reset all local data).

Still crashing? Please contact us with your device model and OS version.

My game data / balance disappeared after an update.

Game data is stored in SharedPreferences (Android) or UserDefaults (iOS). Data is preserved through updates. However, uninstalling and reinstalling the app will erase all local data as this is standard OS behaviour.

If data was lost during an update (not an uninstall), please contact support with your app version details and we'll investigate.

Sound / Text-to-Speech isn't working.

• Check that your device isn't in Silent / Do Not Disturb mode.
• Ensure media volume (not ringtone volume) is turned up.
• Check Settings → Audio in-app to confirm Sound Effects and TTS are enabled.
• On Android: ensure the en_IN (English India) TTS language pack is installed via System Settings → Text-to-Speech.

Haptic vibration isn't working on Android.

BullBear Battle requires the VIBRATE permission to provide haptic feedback. Ensure:
• Settings → Haptics → Vibration is enabled in-app.
• System vibration is not disabled in device settings.
• Your device supports VibrationEffect (API 26+, which all supported devices do).

The candlestick chart is not animating or looks wrong.

• Go to Settings → Display → Animations and ensure animations are enabled.
• Try toggling the setting off and on.
• A chart rendering issue may occur if the app has been in the background for a long time — force-quit and relaunch.

Which devices and OS versions are supported?

iOS: iPhone and iPad running iOS 16.0 or later. Optimised for iPhone 14 and newer.
Android: Devices running Android 8.0 (API 26) or later. Targets Android 15 (API 35). Covers 95%+ of active Android devices.

📢 Ads & Privacy
Why does the app show ads? How often?

BullBear Battle is free to play. Interstitial ads (via Google AdMob) are shown after a set number of completed trades to support continued development. Ads are not shown during active gameplay — only between rounds.

How do I opt out of personalised advertising?

Go to Settings → Privacy → Ad Preferences inside the app to review or withdraw your advertising consent. You can switch from personalised to contextual (non-personalised) ads at any time.

On Android, you can also reset or opt out via Google Settings → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.
On iOS, manage tracking via Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

Does the app collect my personal data?

No personal data is collected by us. All gameplay data is stored locally on your device only. The only data flow to external servers is via Google AdMob for advertising purposes. Please see our full Privacy Policy for details.

I'm in the EU. What about GDPR consent?

On first launch in EU/EEA/UK, the app presents a GDPR consent dialog via Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP). Personalised ads are only enabled if you explicitly consent. You can change this decision at any time in Settings → Privacy → Ad Preferences.

🏆 Achievements & Progression
How many achievements are there and how do I unlock them?

There are 26 achievements in four tiers: Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum. They cover trade count, win streaks, balance milestones, jackpot wins, direction specialisation, and more. Achievements are checked automatically after every trade — no manual claiming required.

What are the 10 career levels?

Your career progresses through 10 levels based on cumulative performance. Each level unlocks new features: chart cosmetic themes, leverage options, and expanded position sizing. Level-up celebrations appear with a full-screen animation when you reach a new tier.

Levels range from Rookie Trader (L1) to Hedge Fund Legend (L10).

Are the leaderboards online or local?

BullBear Battle features a local leaderboard only — no server connection, no comparison with other players. The 6 leaderboard categories (Best Win, Best Streak, Most Profit, Most Trades, Biggest Balance, Best Win Rate) track your personal bests.

My achievement progress reset. Why?

Achievement progress is stored locally. It can reset if you used Settings → Reset Achievements or Reset Everything, or if you uninstalled and reinstalled the app. If neither of those occurred, please contact us with your device details.

What are the 6 Leaderboard categories?

• Best Win — highest single-trade profit (₹)
• Best Streak — longest consecutive win streak
• Most Profit — total accumulated profit
• Most Trades — total trades completed
• Biggest Balance — peak balance ever reached
• Best Win Rate — highest win % (minimum 20 trades)
